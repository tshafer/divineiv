Hangover IV FAQs

Is Hangover IV therapy only for hangovers, or can it be used for other conditions?

Hangover IV therapy at Divine IV and Wellness is designed to alleviate the symptoms of a hangover, but the combination of vitamins, electrolytes, and minerals may also provide support for general hydration and wellness. While its primary use is to aid in hangover recovery, it might be part of a broader health and wellness regimen. Always consult our specialists to determine the best treatments for your unique needs.

Are there any restrictions or guidelines to follow before receiving Hangover IV therapy?

It's advised to consult with our practitioners at Divine IV and Wellness before scheduling a Hangover IV session. While the therapy is generally safe, discussing your medical history, current medications, and any existing health conditions can help ensure that the treatment is appropriate for you. Additionally, it's recommended to eat a light meal and drink water before the session to aid in your comfort and recovery.

Can Hangover IV therapy be customized based on individual needs?

At Divine IV and Wellness, we strive to provide personalized care tailored to your specific needs. If you have particular concerns or preferences regarding the Hangover IV therapy, our experienced team will work with you to adjust the treatment as needed. Whether it's adjusting the combination of nutrients or considering any special health conditions, our goal is to ensure that the therapy aligns with your unique requirements.
